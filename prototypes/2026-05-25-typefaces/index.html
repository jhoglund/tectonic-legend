Typeface And Font Treatment Options

Eight options shown in the same Home composition. The question is not just “which font looks nice,” but which treatment makes the app feel focused, legible, puzzle-native, and warm enough to come back to daily.

1. Inter + JetBrains Mono

Current baseline. Neutral, precise, very readable. The mono board numerals make the puzzle feel technical and serious.

@@PHONE@@

2. Apple System + Rounded Numerals

Most native iOS. Softer board numbers reduce the “developer tool” feel while keeping the rest familiar.

@@PHONE@@

3. IBM Plex Sans

Humanist but still rational. Slightly warmer than Inter, with excellent small text and a calm expert-tool quality.

@@PHONE@@

4. Source Sans 3 + Mono Board

Open, friendly, and high-legibility. More spacious copy; may make the product feel less premium but very approachable.

@@PHONE@@

5. Manrope

Geometric and modern. Stronger personality, bolder headings, but the heavier numerals can feel a little loud.

@@PHONE@@

6. Public Sans + JetBrains Mono

Functional and civic. Great for restrained product UI; less cozy, more “serious daily trainer.”

@@PHONE@@

7. Nunito Sans

Friendliest option. Rounded, sticky, and casual; good retention energy, but risks feeling too game-like.

@@PHONE@@

8. Manrope Display + IBM Plex UI

Hybrid recommendation candidate: expressive page titles, calm readable UI, precise mono puzzle numerals.
