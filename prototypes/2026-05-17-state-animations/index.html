State animations — exploration

Gentle transitions for the moments a control or cell changes state — button states, a number entering the grid, the active-cell ring. Durations are the categorical motion tokens (fast 120ms / base 200ms, ease-out); no overshoot, no springs. Tap any sample to play it. Session: 2026-05-17.

1 · Button state change

Tap a button to toggle its active (e.g. Notes-on) state; press and hold for the pressed state. The variants differ only in how the colour change is timed.

2 · Number enters a cell

A placed number grows into place as its colour settles in. Tap a cell to place a number; tap again to clear and replay.

3 · Active-cell ring

The brand ring eases in when a cell becomes the active one, and the value settles to the selected-cell blue. Tap a cell to toggle.
